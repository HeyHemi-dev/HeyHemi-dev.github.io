Understanding Emotional Intelligence (EQ)

Emotional Intelligence (EQ)—a toolset for the heart and mind; It's all about recognising and assessing emotions, feelings, and thoughts. Think of it as problem-solving but for the less tangible issues of mind and emotion. You know how we talked about solving technical problems, reasoning, and working through knowledge? That's IQ. Now imagine the same thing, but for the less defined, more nebulous challenges—we're now in the realm of EQ.

If IQ is like terminal code—defined, structured, with clear right and wrong answers—then EQ is like a chat with a LLM—it's fuzzy, probabilistic, flexible, and not always perfectly accurate.

Why is EQ important, you might wonder? Well, it's because we're human, and being human is inherently fuzzy. Personal and interpersonal aspects permeate everything we are. Learning to navigate these aspects with skill is crucial, as it helps us better understand ourselves and our relationships.
